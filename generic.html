The Moderator

A moderator is someone who facilitates discussion,
debate, or forum to ensure that participants adhere
to the rules, stay on topic, and engage with one another.

A moderator could be someone who mediates disputes or conflicts between individuals or groups, often impartially to help reach a resolution or consensus.

He/She can also refer to person who oversees and manages the technical aspects of an online platform or community, such as maintaining order, technical issues, and implementing policies.
